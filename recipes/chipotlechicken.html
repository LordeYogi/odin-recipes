Chipotle Chicken

Why Homemade Chipotle Chicken?

I don’t know about you but I LOVE copycat recipes!

I mean, what’s not to love when you get to enjoy a delicious homemade version of your favorite restaurant dish at a fraction of the price that you can, if you wish, make in bulk or vary according to your preferences.

Ingredients

  • 7 oz. Can chipotles in adobo sauce
  • 1 teaspoon cumin
  • 1 teaspoon coarsely ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/4 cup chopped red onion
  • 3 teaspoons minced garlic
  • Juice of 2 limes about 1⁄4 cup
  • 1 teaspoon Kosher salt
  • 4-6 thin-sliced boneless skinless chicken breasts

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients except for the chicken in the bowl of a food processor or blender. Pulse several times to make a marinade paste.
  2. Place chicken breasts and marinade into a gallon size plastic bag and seal closed. Squish around the chicken and marinade so that all the chicken is fully coated. Refrigerate for a minimum of 3 hours.
  3. After chicken has marinated, pre-heat grill on high heat for 15 minutes. Remove chicken from marinade and discard bag. Place chicken on grill and cook on each side for 10-12 minutes or until chicken is cooked to an internal temperature of 165°F.
  4. Adjust grill heat while cooking to ensure that there is a little bit of char, but the meat does not burn or catch on fire.
    5. Remove cooked chicken from the grill and let rest for 5 minutes. Chop into pieces and serve with tacos, rice bowls, salads, etc.
